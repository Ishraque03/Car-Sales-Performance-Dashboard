# Car-Sales-Performance-Dashboard (Using MS Excel)
## Project Overview
This project analyzes **car sales performance data from 2022–2023** using an interactive dashboard to uncover sales trends, revenue drivers, and growth opportunities. The dashboard supports **data-driven decision-making** by providing insights across time, region, customer demographics, manufacturers, models, dealers, and vehicle body styles.

- **Total Records:** 23,906 car sales  
- **Time Period:** 2 Years (2022–2023)
  
## Project Objectives
- Monitor overall sales performance and year-on-year growth  
- Identify top-performing regions, manufacturers, models, and dealers  
- Analyze seasonal trends and customer demographics  
- Support inventory planning and sales strategy optimization

## Key Business Questions Addressed
- What are the **total sales, units sold, and average price** during 2022–2023?
- How do **sales trends change by quarter**, and are there seasonal patterns?
- Which **regions perform best and worst** in terms of revenue?
- How is **sales distributed by customer gender**?
- Which **vehicle body styles and models** drive the highest sales?
- Which **manufacturers and dealers** generate the most revenue?
- How do **engine and transmission preferences** influence sales?
- What **data-driven insights** can support pricing, inventory, and regional strategy decisions?

## Process
1. Collected and cleaned **23,906 car sales records (2022–2023)** using Excel.
2. Performed data preparation:
   - Removed duplicates and handled missing values  
   - Standardized dates, prices, and categories
3. Created key metrics:
   - Total Sales, Cars Sold, Average Price
4. Built pivot tables to analyze:
   - Quarterly trends, regional performance, and customer segments
5. Designed an **interactive Excel dashboard** using:
   - Slicers (Year, Region, Company, Transmission, Engine, Color, Company)
   - Dynamic charts and KPI cards
6. Analyzed patterns to generate **business insights and recommendations**

## Key Insights (2022–2023)
- Generated **$671.5M** in sales from **23,906 cars**, with growth driven by higher volume.
- **2023 outperformed 2022** despite a slightly lower average selling price.
- Sales show strong **seasonality**, peaking consistently in **Q4**.
- **Austin** is the top-performing region; other regions show growth potential.
- Sales are **male-dominated**, though female participation increased year-on-year.
- **Chevrolet, Ford, and Dodge** lead manufacturer performance.
- A small group of **top-selling models** drives a large share of total sales.
- **Rabun Used Car Sales** is the top-performing dealer across both years.
- **Sedans and SUVs** dominate sales, with rising SUV demand.

## Recommendations
- Prioritize inventory for **top-selling models**, especially **SUVs and Sedans**, to improve stock turnover.
- Increase marketing spend and dealer incentives during **Q3–Q4** to capitalize on peak seasonal demand.
- Strengthen dealer and inventory support in **high-performing regions** such as Austin.
- Expand **female-focused marketing and financing strategies** to capture untapped demand.
- Replicate best practices from **top-performing dealers** across mid- and low-performing dealerships.
- Maintain a **competitive, volume-led pricing strategy** while upselling add-ons and services.

## Business Impact
- Improved visibility into **sales performance, trends, and revenue drivers**.
- Enabled **data-driven inventory and regional planning** decisions.
- Identified opportunities for **demand growth and customer segmentation**.
- Supported **strategic decision-making** through a centralized, interactive dashboard.
